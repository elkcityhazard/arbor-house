Welcome To Arbor House Assisted Living Home

We invite you to learn more about Arbor House Assisted Living - our quaint community specializing in personalized care for those with dementia and memory loss. Arbor House has been serving families in the Grand Traverse area for years. Our residents enjoy a carefree lifestyle of personal attention from our team of professional caregivers. Most of our caregivers have been working at Arbor House for many years. The residents cherish the special bond which is created between the caregivers they come to know and trust with their care. Arbor House is located at the base of the Old Mission Peninsula in Traverse City. Close enough to city services and Doctors, yet nestled in the woods off a private cul de sac. For a personal tour and availability, please contact the owner, Cindy Robertson.
