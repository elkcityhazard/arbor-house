Who We Are & Our Experience

Thank you for your interest in Arbor House Assisted Living is located on the Old Mission Peninsula. We are a state licensed Adult Foster Care (AFC) and Assisted Living home serving six to eight seniors. We specialize in dementia care and/or memory care. Arbor House follows the philosophy that everyone is an individual and deserves individual care and concern. Browse our web site for more information.

If you are interested in learning more about our services, or Alzheimer’s Care and Dementia Care in Traverse City Please call us 231-935-3772

We offer long-term care for individuals with Alzheimer's and Dementia in the Traverse City area.

Arbor house is an Assisted Living home specializing in dementia, memory loss and alzheimers. The owner, Cindy Robertson, is a native of the area and has a passion for caregiving. A member of the Michigan Assisted Living Association, Cindy is active with the Bay area Senior Advocates, and provides on-going mentoring and training for her small staff of loyal caregivers. The caregivers are skillful, efficient and caring. Many have been working at Arbor House for years. A well monitored routine schedule ensures that the residents are well groomed, fed, monitored, exercised and engaged... read more about us
